3 Ingredient Cake

“Are you kidding me?” Those are the exact words that will come out of your mouth when you read the ingredients and instructions needed to bake this homemade cake using cake mix and pie filling. It is that easy! 3 eggs, one box of cake mix, and pie filling are all it takes.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 box of cake mix (any flavour)
  • 1 can pie filling (any flavour)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a 9×13 baking pan and line with parchment paper.
  2. Add all 3 ingredients to a large bowl
  3. Mix ingredients together using a spatula or whisk. Mix just until no lumps remain
  4. Use a spatula to spread the batter into the prepared pan, creating an even layer
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes
  6. Carefully remove from oven and allow to cool in the pan completely.
