Kunafa recipe

Kunafa or (Knafeh) is an easy Middle Eastern dessert that is made with a delicious cream (Ashta) filling between two layers of crispy Kunafa (shredded dough). Drizzled with sweet syrup and topped with pistachio slices. It is delicious hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • Thin shredded filo dough
  • Ghee
  • Sugar, vanilla extract, lemon juice, water and glucose liquid
  • Heavy cream, cornflour, vanilla extract, and a small amount of sugar because we will add syrup, and we don't want it to be too sweet. (for making the cream)
  • I use slices of pistachio; however, you can use any variety of nuts you like for the topping
